In the past, our clinical interviews have helped clients in cases involving:

About Dr. Vermilyea

Dr. James Vermilyea
x

James A. Vermilyea, Ph.D., ABPP, Fa.Clin.P., MBA

Dr. Vermilyea currently limits his practice to evaluations for immigration related matters. He has been doing immigration evaluations for over ten years and has completed over 900 evaluations in a broad variety of cases.

Dr. Vermilyea has provided expert testimony in more than 300 civil, criminal and immigration related matters.

Dr. Vermilyea is the director of CBTA and has been a practicing licensed psychologist for over 37 years and is an expert in cognitive behavioral therapy, behavioral therapy and diagnostic consultation. He has been board certified for 28 years, a recognition by his peers of the highest standards.

He trained at McLean Hospital and worked on call at several hospital emergency rooms in Boston. Dr. Vermilyea had been a Clinical Instructor at the Harvard Medical School where he supervised interns and residents. He has supervised post-doctoral students through Boston University and through Tufts Medical School. He was a senior psychologist and assistant to the chairman of Lahey Clinic’s Department of Psychiatry and Behavioral Medicine. His graduate training included studying with some of the world’s preeminent researcher-clinicians in the fields of anxiety disorders and depression.

  • Board Certified in Clinical Psychology
  • American Board of Professional Psychology #4604 (1996)
  • Licensed Psychologist Massachusetts #4233 (1987)
